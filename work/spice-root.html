How it lands on your shelf

From monsoon soil
to your Tuesday curry.

No warehouses, no years of shelf-life. Four steps, eleven days, one jar.

Step 01 · Day 0
i

Harvest

The farmer picks only when the plant asks. We pay premium for imperfect timing.

Step 02 · Day 3
ii

Slow-dry

Sun and shade, never kilns. Moisture drops 72% → 8% over 72 hours.

Step 03 · Day 9
iii

Stone-grind

80-jar batches on Tuesdays. Grinding generates no heat. Flavour stays put.

Step 04 · Day 11
iv

Ship warm

Sealed within 48 hours of grinding. Delivered before the aroma forgets itself.
