Origin Story

Tellicherry (Thalassery) on the Karnataka coast has produced the world's benchmark black pepper for over 400 years. The TGSEB grade — Tellicherry Garbled Special Extra Bold — represents the top 10% of any harvest by berry diameter (4.25mm+). Only berries allowed to ripen to near-red on the vine make this grade.

We hand-sort every lot through 4.25mm sieves on-site before shipping, rejecting any batch where more than 2% undergrade has been mixed in at source. The resulting purity level tests at 99.1% of specification — a figure our lab team is proud of.

How to Use

Culinary Applications.

◈
Finishing Dishes

Add after plating, never during cooking if you want maximum impact. The high volatile oil content evaporates quickly on heat — finishing preserves the citrus-floral compounds that define this grade.

◈
Grilled Meats

A heavy crust of Zero Meridian on aged ribeye before a cast-iron sear creates a bark unlike anything from blended pepper. The woody-citrus notes play directly against rendered Maillard crust.

◈
Classic Pepper Sauce

For a steak au poivre that stands alone, bloom 2 tbsp in butter, deglaze with cognac, add cream. No other pepper handles this reduction technique with the same structural integrity.
