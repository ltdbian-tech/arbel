Kashmiri
Ember Blend

Heat 3/5 Kashmir, India 2,400m Altitude Oct – Nov Harvest FSSAI Certified

“Deep amber heat drawn from slow-roasted Kashmiri chillies, balanced by the cool floral depth of black cardamom and an undertow of aged fennel. Three years in development — and worth every test batch.”

Origin Story

The Kashmiri chillies in this blend are sourced from a single collective of 12 farms in Sopore, where altitude and mineral-rich soil produce pods with unmistakable depth. At 2,400 metres, the growing season is short and the sun intense — critical factors that concentrate volatile oils and develop the characteristic smokiness without added heat.

Each batch is slow-roasted at exactly 140°C for 12 minutes before blending — a process that activates Maillard reactions in the pod walls and creates the dark mahogany colour the blend is known for.

How to Use

Culinary Applications.

◈
Slow-Braised Meats

Rub 2 tsp into lamb shoulder with yoghurt and oil. Marinate 4 hours minimum. The long, slow cook amplifies smokiness and melts the floral notes into the braising liquid.

◈
Kashmiri Shorba

Bloom 1 tsp in hot ghee before adding the broth base. The heat releases rose-petal esters first, giving layers of depth unreachable by any other technique.

◈
Compound Butter

Fold 1.5 tsp into 100g softened butter with a pinch of fleur de sel. Perfect on grilled flatbreads, roasted cauliflower, or as a finishing melt over grilled fish.
