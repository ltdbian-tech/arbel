04 / THE PROCESS

From Soil to
Laboratory.

01
◈

Field Sourcing

Our botanists travel to source farms during peak harvest windows. Every batch is GPS-logged, sampled, and photographed before purchase approval.

02
◈

Lab Analysis

Each intake undergoes 24-point testing: purity, volatile oil content, moisture, microbial count, heavy metals, and pesticide residue screening.

03
◈

Precision Blending

Approved lots enter our temperature-controlled blending chamber. Formulations are executed to 0.1g tolerance using calibrated digital dosing systems.

04
◈

Sealed & Shipped

Nitrogen-flushed, UV-barrier packaging preserves volatile oils for 24 months. Each unit carries a QR code linking to its full lab report.
