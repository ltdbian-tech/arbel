This Privacy Policy describes how Arbel ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our website generator platform at arbel.live (the "Service"). We are committed to transparency about our data practices.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Arbel is a client-side application. The generator runs entirely in your browser. We do not operate application servers that log your activity. However:

  • GitHub Pages hosting: The static site is served via GitHub Pages, which may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, access times) per GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Cloudflare (if applicable): If Cloudflare is used as a CDN/DNS provider, standard web analytics may be collected per Cloudflare's privacy policy.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or physical address
  • We do not store your GitHub token, API keys, or passwords on any server
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • We do not use analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with third parties
  • We do not build user profiles or track behavior across sessions

All data transmission occurs directly from your browser to the respective third-party API. Arbel does not act as an intermediary or proxy for your data, except for the OAuth code exchange which passes through our Cloudflare Worker.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All user data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage and sessionStorage).
  • No user data is transmitted to or stored on Arbel-controlled servers (except the transient OAuth code exchange).
  • Your GitHub PAT and AI API keys never leave your browser except when sent directly to their respective APIs over HTTPS.
  • We recommend using fine-grained GitHub tokens with minimal permissions (repo scope only).
  • You can clear all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for arbel.live.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with the following third parties. Their privacy policies govern their handling of your data:

  • GitHub: GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Google Fonts: Google Privacy Policy
  • Groq (optional): Groq Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini (optional): Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Workers: Cloudflare Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the Service, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

6. Your Rights

Because Arbel operates as a client-side application with no server-side data storage, you have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is in your browser's localStorage — you can inspect it at any time via browser developer tools.
  • Deletion: Clear localStorage for arbel.live to delete all stored data instantly.
  • Portability: Your generated website code is yours. Export it at any time.
  • Revocation: Revoke your GitHub PAT at any time from your GitHub settings. Remove AI API keys from your localStorage.

For users in the EU/EEA (GDPR), California (CCPA), or other jurisdictions with data protection laws: because we do not collect, store, or process personal data on our servers, most data subject rights are inherently satisfied by our architecture. If you have specific concerns, contact us.

7. Data Retention

Arbel does not retain any user data on servers. Browser localStorage persists until you clear it or uninstall your browser. The OAuth worker processes authentication codes transiently and does not log or store them.

8. International Data Transfers

When you use the Service, data is transmitted directly from your browser to third-party APIs (GitHub, Google, Groq) which may be located in different countries. These transfers are governed by the respective third parties' privacy policies and data transfer mechanisms.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
